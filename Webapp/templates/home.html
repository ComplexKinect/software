Welcome to Complex Kinect!

Complex Kinect is a team of 5 students at Olin College of Engineering. The goal of our project is integrate elements of mechanical, electrical and software design by constructing an interactive structure of nodes that responds to motion or sound in its environment depending on a user's input.

Our webapp allows a user to select between response to motion, response to sound, or demo mode.
